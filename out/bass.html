Electric Bass

If You or someone You know in the South Jersey Area is looking for a Bassist who is willing and able to play almost any style of music, especially on the short term, please do not hesitate to Contact Me today.

My journey on the bass has been one fraught with indecision and strife, most of which was completely unnecessary. I took quite a long time to figure out that the electric guitar was something which annoyed me to play, and continuously avoided the idea of the bass on the grounds that it was “too simple.” I was completely wrong.

I know this now, and absolutely adore playing bass. I have an Ibanez Gio Soundgear Bass (a red one), tuned to DGCF (That gives me a better range for the pieces I generally play, with a sacrifice of only a few never used notes.) which I used to play fairly often. I loved every moment of it, and find that what I once considered to be a limited number of strings to allow creativity I just cannot achieve when playing on a six string electric. In a word, the Electric bass was “right” for me.

In the past few months, I kinda got away from playing bass. I was frustrated with the difficulties of finding people to play with (it is so much harder to do than it is with my harp…) and other aspects of my life were in desperate need of my attention. So, in an effort to make myself start playing again (and to keep myself a bit more honest about how much and how often I practice on the bass) I have decided to put my entire repertoire up on this site.
