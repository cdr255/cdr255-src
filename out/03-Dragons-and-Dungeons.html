NA03. Dragons and Dungeons

Prelude

From NA02, the party had decided to aid Liana’s Rebellion instead of the Resistance. They travelled to Gariland, upon the word of Liana, before she fled.

The following is the first part, which I did by improvisation, because my printer was dead at the time: They found Liana at the stale potion, they accepted a job offer to go and retrieve “an important object,” travelled west for a long time (stopping to have some weapons made and hide out from the Empire a bit), reached the Rat Cellar, Found an object which fit the bill, helped the people of the Rat Cellar defend themselves against the Empire’s siege, and then headed East again.

Now, they must meet up with Liana, and somehow break Dorian out of his cell before he is executed.

Meta Info

This is an adventure module for a Heavily Modified version of 3.5 edition Dungeons and Dragons. It is intended for 4-5 5th Level Characters, and was specifically written as part of an ongoing campaign entitled, “End of the Empire.”

Post Combat Oppurtunities

Hopefully, they will not wanna torch the rest of the village. If they do, draw Good-Aligned characters into suspicion of Evil. Then, they must find their way back to the vicinity of the Duke’s Fort. Otherwise, dip into random shit.

The Fun Begins

Once they are within an hour of the fort, they trigger “Liana’s Greater Warding of Alarm,” a 7th level divination spell to alert her when the party is back in the vicinity. Within a little time, have her arrive in their place on foot. She speaks, saying that the plan is already underway to break Dorian out of prison and flee to a safer place for a time. Had they gotten their sooner, they could have helped with the infiltration… but since they are here, they can clear out the escape route.

The Duke’s dungeons have been connected to an old mine by a subterranian tunnel, and they are going to usher Dorian out that way. However, the Mines are unfortunately forgotten to the empire, which means they have been infested with some creatures of ill repute: Kobolds, of the Golbadin Tribe, some days journey to the east. They must clear out the dungeon.
